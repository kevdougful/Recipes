#Mac & Cheese (Stove Top)

####Ingredients
- 1 lb elbow macaroni
- 1/4 cup butter
- 2 cup milk
- 1/4 cup flour
- 2 cup shredded jack cheese
- 1 tsp minced garlic

####Directions
1. Cook pasta
2. While pasta cooks, melt butter in medium pot/sauce pan.  Add garlic to melted butter and sautee slightly.
3. Whisk flour to melted butter thoroughly
4. Slowly add milk to butter/flour mixture (roux).  Heat to boiling point.
5. Add cheese.
6. Drain pasta and combine with sauce.
